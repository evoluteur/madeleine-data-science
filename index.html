Proportion of flour, sugar, butter, and eggs in Madeleine cakes

The 4 main ingredients of madeleines are flour, sugar, butter, and eggs. With a little yeast or baking soda these are all you need to make classic madeleines.

These 4 ingredients are found in most madeleine recipes, but as seen in the 147 Madeleines recipes on Marmiton.com their proportions can vary widely.
